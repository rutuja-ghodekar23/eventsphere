Connecting Passions,

EventSphere is India's leading platform for college students and professionals to discover, register, and experience world-class technical, cultural, and sports events.

Our Mission

To make event discovery seamless and ensure no talent misses out on life-changing opportunities due to a lack of awareness.

Our Community

We connect thousands of students across top colleges in Pune, Mumbai, Bangalore, Nagpur, and Delhi with premier event organizers.

Our Impact

From massive hackathons to national debate championships, EventSphere has facilitated over 100,000+ seamless event registrations.

Built For The Future

Founded in 2024, EventSphere started as a small college project to solve a simple problem: finding out what's happening on campus. Today, it has grown into a robust, full-stack event management platform powering events across multiple metropolises. Our technology ensures a smooth registration process, real-time analytics for organizers, personalized event recommendations, comprehensive feedback system, and enterprise-grade data validation.
