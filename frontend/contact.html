Frequently Asked

How do I register for an event?
Create an account, browse events, click View Details, then Register Now. You'll get a confirmation on screen and via email.
Can I cancel my registration?
Yes! Go to your Dashboard → Upcoming Events → Cancel Registration. Please cancel at least 24 hours before the event starts.
Is EventSphere free to use?
Yes! EventSphere is completely free for students and professionals. Sign up and start exploring events across 5 major cities in India.
How do I become an event organizer?
Email us at support@eventsphere.com with your college details and event proposal. Our team will review and grant organizer access within 48 hours.
What cities are covered?
We currently cover Pune, Mumbai, Nagpur, Bangalore, and Delhi — with 35+ events happening across these cities. More cities coming soon!
